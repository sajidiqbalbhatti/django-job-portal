Privacy Policy

TalentBridge values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. Read below to understand how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Introduction

TalentBridge is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe online experience. We only collect personal information necessary to provide our services effectively and securely.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, password, profile details.
  • Job Search Activity: Jobs viewed, saved, applied to.
  • Communication Data: Messages, support tickets, interactions.
  • Usage Data: IP address, device information, browser type for analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To personalize your job search and recommendations.
  • To communicate updates and support messages.
  • To protect users and platform security.

Sharing & Disclosure

  • With partner companies to provide job opportunities.
  • To comply with legal obligations or lawful requests.
  • To prevent fraud and protect platform integrity.

Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Choices

  • Update or delete your profile information anytime.
  • Opt out of promotional emails.
  • Manage cookie preferences via browser settings.
